What We Do

Our Core Services

Comprehensive telecom infrastructure services delivered with precision, safety, and speed.

Tower Erection & Installation

Ground-based towers, rooftop structures, and monopoles — installed with precision and adherence to industry safety standards.

  • GBT & RTT Towers
  • Monopoles & Lattice Towers
  • Foundation & Civil Works

BTS / NE Installation

End-to-end Base Transceiver Station deployment for 4G & 5G networks — antenna mounting, feeder cabling, power integration.

  • Antenna Alignment & Mounting
  • Feeder & Jumper Installation
  • Power Integration

Optical Fiber Cable (OFC) Laying

Underground and aerial OFC laying across urban and semi-urban landscapes with minimal disruption and maximum durability.

  • Underground HDD & Trenching
  • Aerial OFC Stringing
  • Splicing & Testing

FTTH / Last Mile Connectivity

Fiber-to-the-Home deployment connecting end users to high-speed broadband networks through reliable last-mile infrastructure.

  • FTTH Network Design
  • ONT/OLT Installation
  • Last-Mile Fiber Drop

Network O&M Services

Round-the-clock operations and maintenance ensuring uptime, performance, and quick fault resolution across your telecom network.

  • Preventive Maintenance
  • Fault Resolution & Rollout
  • 24×7 Field Support

Power & Energy Solutions

Reliable power solutions for telecom sites — solar integration, DG sets, battery systems and energy audits for uninterrupted operations.

  • Solar Power Systems
  • Battery Backup & DG
  • Energy Auditing
